From Local to Global

Dumaguete City, a thriving student hub with 15,000 to 20,000 students, is a gathering place for future leaders from across the nation and the world. As they make this city their home, we have the unique responsibility to equip them for the greater mission of bringing the gospel to their communities and nations of the world.

By investing in the next generation, we are raising leaders and campus missionaries, and to advance this vision, we are building a church facility as a hub for discipleship and leadership development.
